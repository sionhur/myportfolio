Desert Objects

Zine | Furniture Design | Writing
December 2022
See More

Desert Objects features a collection of furniture pieces that I designed, each accompanied by its own unique story. Each piece of furniture is inspired by a specific aspect of the desert, the very place where I spent my formative years. As I reflect on my memories of the isolated, arid, and sweltering desert, these creations serve as a nostalgic connection to cherished moments.

These pieces not only evoke personal nostalgia but also invite the observer to immerse themselves in the essence of that space. It is my hope that these pieces not only resonate with my personal experiences but also resonate with those who encounter them, enabling them to connect with and appreciate the evocative nature of the desert.
