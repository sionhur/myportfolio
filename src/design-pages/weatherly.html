Weatherly

App UX/UI | Development by Sehee Hyung
Mar 2020
See More
Have you ever been annoyed by constantly having to switch between the calendar and weather apps to plan an event? With the weather-based scheduler, you can have everything in one place.
This app is designed for anyone who wants to plan events based on the weather. It features a week-based calendar that displays the weather summary for each day, including the current, low, and high temperatures, sunrise and sunset times, as well as humidity and wind speed. Each day is further divided into hours, with each hour represented by a weather icon and its corresponding temperature. Moreover, the app automatically synchronizes data from the default calendar on your phone and integrates it into its own calendar.
