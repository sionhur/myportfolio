Seeing through Vessels

Exhibition Design | Model Making
April 2023
See More

Seeing Through Glass is an exhibition showcasing a captivating collection of glass vessels from various Western civilizations, spanning ancient times to contemporary art. The exhibition design emphasizes the inherent “transparency” of these vessels, enabling viewers to peer through one piece of glass and into another, connecting these timeless works.

From the exterior, observers catch glimpses of the vibrant and valuable glass material through the mesh fabric of the title wall and the opening on the right. These visual cues beckon them toward a space brimming with lightness. To enhance the airy atmosphere and accentuate the brilliance of the glass objects, the exhibition employs muted cool colors, a simple rounded font, and minimal graphic elements. This combination serves to elevate the space and create a sense of openness, while allowing the vibrant colors of the glass pieces to take center stage.
