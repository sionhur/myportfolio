Ikea Home Cloud Concept

UI/UX Design | Product Design
April 2023
See More

Ikea Home Cloud is a home hub concept envisioned for the Swedish home goods brand, Ikea. Ikea is widely recognized and appreciated worldwide for its friendly and accessible brand image. The home hub design comprises the Cloud Speaker (Moln), the Moon Light (MÃ¥ne), and a connected app that enables control over various smart home features. This whimsical design not only serves as a speaker but also functions as a lamp and a sculptural object, imbuing the home with a delightful sense of joy. The elongated cloud's elegant shape seamlessly integrates into any space, creating a warmer and more inviting atmosphere.

The accompanying app empowers users to manage the home hub using their smartphones. It provides convenient control over various settings within the house, including lighting, temperature regulation, television, music, and home security, among others. The app's design follows the same light and minimalistic approach as the product and the Ikea brand itself. The main colors utilized are Ikea's signature blue and yellow, complemented by light shades that enhance the app's friendly and animated feel. At the bottom, a circular shape represents the Moon Light, allowing users to adjust lighting settings while also serving as a control dial for other settings.
