Sion Hur

Multidisciplinary Designer & Artist
BFA Tufts University

In my mixed media artworks and product design, I explore themes of "home" and "rest." Constant migrations throughout my childhood have instilled in me an appreciation for a place of comfort and warmth associated with a sense of belonging. Spending time with and closely observing nature, objects, and people is essential to my process. In this process, the interaction with the subject matter becomes one of embrace and understanding rather than of use. I seek to reflect this kind of attitude in my work, whether in design or art, and translate it into spaces and forms, inviting viewers to share in the experience of sanctuary and tranquility.
