Sion Hur

Multidisciplinary Designer & Artist
BFA Tufts University

I explore topics regarding the sense of "home" and "rest" in my mixed media artworks and product design.Constant migrations throughout my childhood across the globe led me to find my place in the world as an observer, a traveler, and a responsible inhabitant. Spending time with and closely observing nature, objects, and people is essential to my process. The interaction with the subject matter becomes one of embrace and understanding rather than of use. I seek to reflect this kind of attitude in my work, whether in design or art, and translate this shared intimacy into spaces and forms that discover newness within familiarity.
